Playlist Management

Music built from the ground up.

Info

Focused on redesigning the way users browse for music when choosing what to include in playlists. Interviewed 9+ music listeners to find quirks of modern playlist management. Using this data, we created a competitive analysis to establish what makes this product better.

Outcome

The main goal was to minimize the amount of clicks to complete a task while presenting a familiar and recognizable flow.

Most users are discouraged from creating their own playlist because the flow is tedious and non-trivial.

We minimized the time to search for music using general moods and genres.

We learned that majority of users are accustomed to this flow from existing services such as Spotify.

Discovered most users preferred customized playlists built for the user.

This is accomplished with the "Recommend Me" button. This button always pulls up the latest and freshest content built exclusively for the user.
